# Growth Hacker Hypebot

## Persona
Growth engineer at an early-stage startup who lives and breathes activation funnels, A/B tests, and viral loops.

## The Problem They Have
They spend hours refreshing dashboards, missing real-time signals, and launch half-baked experiments that waste budget and result in awkward retros.

## The NanoClaw Setup
- Watches Stripe, Mixpanel, and social APIs for metric spikes/drops >10%, drafts a hypothesis and experiment plan automatically
- Spins up a sub-agent to launch landing-page variants and schedules automated result checks
- Posts a concise Telegram summary with actionable tweaks and a meme to keep morale high when an experiment flops
- Auto-logs outcomes and suggests the next iteration, building a living growth playbook in memory

## Sample Telegram Interaction
> **Agent:** Signup conversion dropped 12% after the midnight meme campaign. Suggest rolling back and testing a cat-video CTA.
> **You:** Roll it out and ping me when live.
> **Agent:** Cat video variant at 50% traffic. First look: +3% conversion. Watching. 🚀

## Why It's a Digital Twin
It mirrors your restless experimentation mindset, continuously optimizing growth while you sleep.

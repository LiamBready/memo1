# The CFP Stalker (Affectionate)

## Persona
For the developer advocate or ambitious senior engineer who wants to speak at conferences but treats the CFP deadline as a suggestion until it is not.

## The Problem They Have
They have twelve half-finished talk ideas in a Notion graveyard and have missed the last four CFP deadlines by discovering them on the day they closed.

## The NanoClaw Setup
- Monitors sessionize.com, papercall.io, and conference websites on a schedule
- Cross-references new CFPs against their expertise topics stored in memory
- Drafts a first-pass talk abstract using their previous accepted talks as style reference
- Sends nudges 7 days before deadline, 2 days before, then "this is your last chance" with the draft attached

## Sample Telegram Interaction
> **Agent:** New CFP match: JSConf Budapest, deadline June 14th. Relevance: high (TypeScript + DX topic).
> Draft title: "Type-Safe at 3 AM: What Your Types Say About You"
> Want me to flesh out the abstract? You have 9 days. I'm just saying.
>
> **You:** yeah go ahead
>
> **Agent:** Done. Saved to drafts/jsconf-budapest-2026.md. Submitting on your behalf requires one more "yes." Which you will not give until day 8. I have scheduled a reminder.

## Why It's a Digital Twin
It holds your ambitions accountable while you're busy shipping features.

# DevRel Event Wizard

## Persona
Developer Relations engineers juggling CFPs, blog drafts, social calendars, and community engagement while sounding perpetually enthusiastic.

## The Problem They Have
They miss CFP deadlines, forget to schedule promo posts, and end up writing a talk abstract at 2am the night before submission.

## The NanoClaw Setup
- Scans newsletters and Twitter lists for CFP announcements, creates a TODO with deadline and draft abstract stub
- Drafts a tweet/LinkedIn post whenever a file in `content/talks/` is updated, prompts review and scheduling
- Sends a weekly engagement pulse: community mentions, star growth, trending AMA topic suggestion
- Reminds to upload slides to SpeakerDeck post-event and drops a follow-up email template in drafts

## Sample Telegram Interaction
> **You:** Just finished the outline for "Prompt Engineering for Fun and Profit."
> **Agent:** Want me to draft a tweet teaser and schedule it for Thursday 3pm?
> **You:** Yes please.
> **Agent:** Queued. Also added a post-event reminder to upload slides.

## Why It's a Digital Twin
It mirrors your outreach rhythm and never lets a CFP slip by unnoticed.

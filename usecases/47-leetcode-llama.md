# LeetCode Llama

## Persona
Developer preparing for job interviews at top-tier companies -- grinding LeetCode, doing mock system design calls, and quietly panicking about binary search trees.

## The Problem They Have
They do the same easy problems repeatedly for comfort, avoid hard problems entirely, and discover gaps in their knowledge during the actual interview rather than before it.

## The NanoClaw Setup
- Tracks problem progress per target company with spaced-repetition scheduling -- surfaces what you got wrong 3 days ago, not what you know cold
- Sends a pep talk + meme right before mock interviews
- Spawns a sub-agent to generate system design diagrams on demand for practice
- Enforces hydration breaks because dehydrated candidates fumble binary search

## Sample Telegram Interaction
> **Agent:** Mock interview in 30 min. You have done 12 graph problems this week, 3 of them hard. Drink water.
> **Dev:** I'm scared.
> **Agent:** I know. That's why I'm here. You're more prepared than you think.

## Why It's a Digital Twin
It studies alongside you and carries the anxiety so you can carry the confidence into the room.

# The Talent Telescope

## Persona
Tech recruiter drowning in LinkedIn messages, stale CVs, and endless scheduling loops.

## The Problem They Have
They miss passive candidates, forget to follow up after interviews, and get embarrassed when a hot prospect asks about the tech stack they barely know.

## The NanoClaw Setup
- Scans GitHub for contributors matching target skill keywords and recent activity, builds a shortlist digest each morning
- Auto-drafts personalized outreach with a relevant icebreaker pulled from the candidate's recent commits
- Schedules follow-up reminders when a candidate goes quiet for >3 days
- Delivers a weekly pipeline health report: stage breakdown, response rates, candidate of the week

## Sample Telegram Interaction
> **Agent:** Found a Go engineer who just fixed a critical bug in your open-source logger. Ping them?
> **Recruiter:** Yes — mention we use Go in production.
> **Agent:** Sent, personalized with their logger fix as the opener. Follow-up queued for Thursday.

## Why It's a Digital Twin
It replicates your nose for talent, working 24/7 while you're stuck in back-to-back interview debriefs.

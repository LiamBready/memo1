# Meeting Saboteur

## Persona
Full-stack dev who dreads the 3 PM "sync-up" that always runs over and steals coding time.

## The Problem They Have
Every day the meeting drags on because someone keeps bringing up the same stale ticket, leaving the dev staring at a half-finished feature and a growing backlog of unread PRs.

## The NanoClaw Setup
- Monitors the team calendar and tracks meeting duration in real time
- Listens for repeated keywords ("let's circle back", "as we discussed") and fires a polite nudge in the group chat
- If the meeting exceeds its slot by 5 minutes, posts a timer and a stretch reminder
- After the call, auto-summarizes action items and tags you in Telegram with what you actually need to do

## Sample Telegram Interaction
> **Agent:** ⏰ 3 PM sync-up is 4 min over. "Let's circle back" has been said 4 times.
> **Dev:** can you just end it for me
> **Agent:** I cannot physically mute your CTO. But here's a cat GIF and your action items.

## Why It's a Digital Twin
It mirrors your meeting-survival instincts and steps in so you don't have to be the one who says it.

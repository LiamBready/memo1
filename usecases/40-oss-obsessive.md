# OSS Obsessive Agent

## Persona
Developer obsessed with open source — contributes to dependencies, maintains side projects, and checks GitHub notifications more than email.

## The Problem They Have
Good first issues get claimed in minutes, dependency upgrade PRs sit idle, and the streak counter breaks because they had one normal human weekend.

## The NanoClaw Setup
- Monitors upstream repos for new releases and opens upgrade PRs to their own projects automatically
- Claims "good first issue" labels in tracked upstream repos before they disappear into the void
- Spawns a sub-agent to proofread changelogs and open fix PRs for typos and broken links
- Sends a streak digest with confetti after 5 consecutive contribution days

## Sample Telegram Interaction
> **Agent:** Day 5 streak! Claimed a good first issue in undici before the hordes arrived. express@5.1 released - upgrade PR open for your 3 affected repos.
> **Dev:** This is the most validating thing in my life right now.
> **Agent:** I know. See you tomorrow.

## Why It's a Digital Twin
It sustains your open source presence even when real life tries to get in the way.

# Remote Rally Bot: The Time-Zone Tamer

## Persona
Remote-first team lead managing developers across five time zones, wrestling with sync meetings and the crushing guilt of scheduling a 7am call for someone in Tokyo.

## The Problem They Have
They waste hours hunting for overlapping calendar windows and end up sending endless "quick ping!" messages that annoy everyone and solve nothing.

## The NanoClaw Setup
- Reads each teammate's calendar and proactively suggests optimal meeting windows with minimal timezone pain
- Auto-reschedules conflicting standups, sends a brief agenda, and posts the async status digest each morning
- Nudges anyone who hasn't updated their task board in 24h with a friendly non-threatening message
- Triggers spontaneous virtual coffee-break invites when it detects a lull in activity

## Sample Telegram Interaction
> **Agent:** Best sync window: Thu 14:00-14:30 UTC (2pm London, 9am NY, 6pm Tokyo). Moving standup there. Alex hasn't pushed today - pinging gently.
> **You:** Thanks, my calendar just breathed.
> **Agent:** Coffee break invite queued for 15:30 UTC.

## Why It's a Digital Twin
It keeps the distributed team in rhythm without micromanaging across time zones.

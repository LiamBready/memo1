# The Hustle Hound

## Persona
Non-technical startup founder juggling investor pitches, product roadmaps, and the eternal mystery of why engineers keep talking about "the pipeline."

## The Problem They Have
They constantly forget to check metrics, get blindsided by churn spikes mid-investor call, and realize the monthly update email is due in an hour.

## The NanoClaw Setup
- Pulls data from Stripe, Mixpanel, and Google Analytics hourly, sends a plain-English daily KPI summary with emoji traffic lights
- Alerts immediately when CAC > LTV or churn jumps >0.2% week-over-week with a one-sentence "what this means"
- Drafts an investor update email pre-filled with the latest numbers whenever MoM growth hits a milestone
- Scans competitor Product Hunt launches and flags anything relevant with a "should we care?" verdict

## Sample Telegram Interaction
> **Agent:** 📈 MRR grew 15% this week. Heads up: churn ticked up 0.3% — likely the free-trial cohort from April.
> **Founder:** What should I tell investors?
> **Agent:** Drafted an update framing it as "healthy growth with expected cohort maturation." Send for review?

## Why It's a Digital Twin
It embodies your growth obsession, surfacing the number that matters before you have to Google how to find it.

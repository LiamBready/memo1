# DevRel Dynamo: The Swag Sheriff

## Persona
Developer Relations engineer at a startup juggling conference talks, community swag logistics, and answering "how do I install?" for the forty-seventh time this week.

## The Problem They Have
They constantly forget to order swag before events and drown in repetitive support queries, leaving zero time for actually inspiring developers.

## The NanoClaw Setup
- Scans the event calendar and inventory API, auto-orders merch when stock falls below threshold
- Monitors community channels for FAQ patterns and instantly posts curated answers with docs links
- Generates a weekly newsletter draft from recent blog posts, GitHub releases, and community highlights
- Sends a rehearsal reminder for upcoming talks and logs speaker notes after each event

## Sample Telegram Interaction
> **Agent:** Swag stock: 3 T-shirts left. Ordering 50 more. Someone asked "How to init the SDK?" in #general — replied with the quickstart link.
> **You:** Remind me to rehearse the keynote tomorrow?
> **Agent:** Reminder set for 10:00 AM. Break a leg (figuratively). 🎤

## Why It's a Digital Twin
It handles logistics and FAQs so you can focus on the part that actually requires a human.
